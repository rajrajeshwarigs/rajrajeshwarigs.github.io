Nike | Digital Marketing Performance Analysis

In this project, we used Google Analytics to analyze Nike's e-commerce performance, focusing on revenue, conversion, and user engagement. The data revealed a 76% year-over-year revenue increase, driven by direct and referral channels, and a 39% rise in conversion rates with a strong gain in first-time purchasers. Browser and device insights from GA helped us identify performance gaps—especially high bounce rates on mobile, tablets, and certain browsers like Safari In-App. Based on these findings, we recommended UX improvements, A/B testing, and better attribution modeling. The project showcased how Google Analytics can drive strategic, data-informed marketing decisions.
